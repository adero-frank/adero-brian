Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal services tailored to your specific needs. Explore our areas of expertise and discover how we can assist you.

Corporate Law

Strategic counsel for businesses navigating complex commercial landscapes. I provide comprehensive legal support to ensure your business operations comply with Kenyan regulations while maximizing growth opportunities.

Services Include:

  • • Business formation and incorporation
  • • Mergers and acquisitions
  • • Corporate governance and compliance
  • • Contract drafting and negotiation
  • • Regulatory compliance and licensing
  • • Shareholder agreements and disputes

Why It Matters:

Proper corporate legal structure protects your business assets, ensures regulatory compliance, and provides a solid foundation for growth. Whether you're starting a new venture or expanding an existing one, expert corporate counsel is essential for long-term success.

Civil Litigation

Aggressive representation in high-stakes disputes. I handle complex civil litigation matters with a focus on achieving favorable outcomes through strategic negotiation and, when necessary, vigorous courtroom advocacy.

Services Include:

  • • Contract disputes and breach of contract
  • • Tort claims and personal injury
  • • Commercial disputes
  • • Debt recovery and collection
  • • Property disputes
  • • Alternative dispute resolution (ADR)

Why It Matters:

When disputes arise, having experienced litigation counsel can mean the difference between protecting your rights and suffering significant losses. I work to resolve matters efficiently while protecting your interests and minimizing disruption to your business or personal life.

Family Law

Compassionate legal guidance during life's most challenging moments. I provide sensitive, strategic representation in family law matters, always prioritizing the best interests of children and families.

Services Include:

  • • Divorce and separation proceedings
  • • Child custody and visitation rights
  • • Child support and maintenance
  • • Property division and asset distribution
  • • Estate planning and wills
  • • Adoption and guardianship

Why It Matters:

Family law matters are deeply personal and emotionally charged. Having an experienced advocate who understands both the legal complexities and the human impact ensures that your rights are protected while working toward solutions that serve your family's long-term well-being.

Real Estate

Expert guidance through property transactions and disputes. I help clients navigate the complexities of real estate law in Kenya, ensuring secure transactions and protecting property rights.

Services Include:

  • • Property purchase and sale agreements
  • • Conveyancing and title transfers
  • • Lease agreements and tenancy disputes
  • • Property boundary disputes
  • • Land use and zoning matters
  • • Real estate development agreements

Why It Matters:

Real estate transactions involve significant financial investments and complex legal requirements. Proper legal representation ensures that your property rights are protected, transactions are legally sound, and potential disputes are avoided or resolved effectively.

Intellectual Property

Protecting your innovations and creative assets in the digital age. I help individuals and businesses safeguard their intellectual property rights through comprehensive protection strategies.

Services Include:

  • • Trademark registration and protection
  • • Copyright registration and enforcement
  • • Patent applications and prosecution
  • • IP licensing agreements
  • • IP infringement disputes
  • • Trade secret protection

Why It Matters:

In today's knowledge-based economy, intellectual property is often a company's most valuable asset. Proper IP protection prevents unauthorized use, maintains competitive advantage, and can generate significant revenue through licensing. Protecting your IP is essential for long-term business success.

Employment Law

Comprehensive employment law services for both employers and employees. I provide guidance on workplace rights, obligations, and dispute resolution under Kenyan labor law.

Services Include:

  • • Employment contract drafting and review
  • • Workplace policies and procedures
  • • Wrongful termination and unfair dismissal
  • • Discrimination and harassment claims
  • • Wage and hour disputes
  • • Labor relations and collective bargaining

Why It Matters:

Employment relationships are governed by complex laws and regulations. For employers, proper legal compliance prevents costly disputes and maintains productive workplaces. For employees, understanding your rights ensures fair treatment and appropriate compensation. Expert employment law counsel protects both parties' interests.
